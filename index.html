How to play the game

Cow Bull is mental game where in the rules are very simple. A person(in this case computer) chooses a random 4 letter word and you're to guess the word by looking at the bulls and cows.
A cow is when a letter exists in the original word but not exactly in the same position. A bulls is when a letter exists in the same position of that of the original word.
